How it works

Three steps to find your zone

The quiz uses an adaptive engine — it adjusts in real-time to zero in on your level. No studying needed, just answer honestly.

1
Pick a track
Choose one of the 4 competency areas — database, JVM, solution design, or cloud.
2
Answer questions
The quiz adapts as you go — getting harder when you're right, easier when you're not. Around 20 questions total.
3
Get placed
You'll see your level and a breakdown of scenario strengths, ready for group placement.
